Service overview

We offer crew manpower services. Our mission is to provide high-quality crew on a global scale. We supply qualified crew for all ranks and vessels for the shipping industry, for the ship operators, and for the seafarers.

We know very well how important it is to provide the most reliable and responsive service for your needs, as well as to fill every available position at sea and on shore with the right personnel on time.

Clear Customer Value

In the realm of Manning services, SAMC strives to deliver clear customer value by providing not just a workforce, but a dedicated team of qualified and trained professionals for the shipping industry. Our commitment goes beyond mere recruitment, focusing on ensuring that each crew member is equipped with the necessary skills, safety training, and global standard procedures. This approach not only meets but exceeds the expectations of our clients, fostering a relationship built on trust, reliability, and the assurance of a competent and secure maritime operation.

Experience Matters

In the world of Manning services, SAMC recognizes that experience matters. Our commitment to delivering exceptional maritime manpower goes hand in hand with the value of seasoned expertise. We understand the importance of having a crew with a wealth of experience, ensuring that they not only meet but exceed industry standards. This emphasis on experience brings a heightened level of competence, reliability, and efficiency to our services. By prioritizing skilled professionals with a proven track record, SAMC ensures that our clients benefit from a team well-versed in the intricacies of the shipping industry, ultimately contributing to the success and smooth operation of maritime endeavours.
