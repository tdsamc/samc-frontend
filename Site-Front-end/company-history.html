Company history

SALEM AL MAKRANI CARGO CO has been providing their service in Maritime transportation, Cargo transportation handling, Agency operations, Warehousing and Crew Manning for more than two decades since its establishment in 2003. With the decades of expertise in the industry and skilled human resources with relevant expertise in the shipping industry, the company owns RO/RO Ships, Multipurpose ships, Passenger-cum-Cargo ships and landing crafts that can accommodate various customer requirements.

  • 2003 Start in Dubai, United Arab Emirates

    SAMC was established in the year 2003 as a leading company in the Marine and Off-Shore services with its head quarter in Dubai, United Arab Emirates and a branch office in Fujairah, United Arab Emirates.
